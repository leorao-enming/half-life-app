# Half-Life Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Half-Life is a wellness app that provides estimates about caffeine intake and sleep timing. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

## Data stored on your device

Half-Life stores drink entries, estimates, settings, and any optional drink photos locally on your device. Drink photos are not uploaded by Half-Life.

## Apple Health

Apple Health access is optional. After you choose to connect Apple Health, Half-Life may read heart-rate and sleep data to personalize a caffeine-clearance estimate. Half-Life does not upload the Apple Health data it reads.

## Optional cloud sync

If you sign in to a build with cloud sync enabled, Half-Life may store your drink entries and notes with your account to sync them between your devices. This data is used solely to provide cloud sync. It is not sold, used for advertising, or used for cross-app tracking.

## Your choices

You can remove local entries in the app or delete the app to remove local data. If you use cloud sync, request deletion of your account and synced data through the support contact listed in the App Store or TestFlight test information.

## Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, use the support contact published with the Half-Life App Store or TestFlight listing.
